WEEK 01

You will be able to understand JavaScript’s role in the bigger context of web development and how it works alongside HTML and CSS when rendering a website.

Topics:

  • - Intro Valerie & Your Majesty
  • - Why learn JavaScript?
  • - What does JavaScript do?
  • - The Browser Painting Game
  • - Get the bike out of the canal (p5.js)

INTRODUCTION

WEEK 02

You will get introduced to how you tell JavaScript ‘what to do’. You will playfully experience how a function works and draw a pizza-picture with JavaScript.

Topics:

  • - What is a variable?
  • - What is a function?
  • - What are arguments?
  • - ‘You can be a function’ Game
  • - Draw your favorite pizza (p5.js)

BASICS OF JAVASCRIPT I

WEEK 03

You will learn more about the concepts of JavaScript and different ways how to structure and think about your code. You will practise how to create and write a class and how to use it.

Topics:

  • - What is an object?
  • - What is a class?
  • - What is object-oriented development?

BASICS OF JAVASCRIPT II

WEEK 04

You will learn about the pitfalls of JavaScript and potential frustrations you might encounter. We have time to dive into questions and you will get further tips and resources for your JavaScript learning journey..

Topics:

  • - Most common mistakes
  • - How to debug JavaScript
  • - How to continue learning JavaScript
  • - Helpful links & ressources
  • - Questions & Closing Drinks

THE DARK SIDES OF JAVASCRIPT

Learn from a teacher who understands what you are going through

Valerie has been working as full-time developer at Your Majesty for 1.5 years. Most days she is involved in projects built with JavaScript or JavaScript libraries like React and Node.js. Your Majesty is a digital agency creating brand and product experiences for clients such as Netflix, adidas and Samsung SmartThings.

Being a self-taught developer Valerie enjoys sharing her knowledge and helping other code-enthusiasts on their learning journey. Early 2018 she launched her own Meet-up series Cafe Robot, targeted towards code-newbies in Amsterdam and offering a platform to connect and discuss. She has also been a Meet-up speaker for Girl Code and Contentful as well as a Technology Mentor for Digital School Hyper Island.
